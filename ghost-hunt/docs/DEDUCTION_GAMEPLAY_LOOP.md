# Deduction Gameplay Loop - Skill-Based Problem Solving

## üéØ Purpose

This document describes the **complete gameplay loop** for identifying ghosts using the balanced overlap system. Every ghost requires **2-3 pieces of evidence minimum** to identify, creating skill-based problem-solving gameplay.

---

## üîÑ Core Deduction Loop (Step-by-Step)

### 1. Enter the Hotspot
- Sanity begins ticking down slowly
- Ghost is static at a location (MVP)
- Player must physically walk to find it

---

### 2. Radar First ‚Üí Find the Direction
- Radar blip wiggles ¬±5¬∞ so player must rotate phone IRL
- **Radar gives zero deduction, just direction**
- Player notes the heading (e.g., "Ghost is at 45¬∞")

---

### 3. EMF Second ‚Üí Confirm Distance + Observe Personality
- EMF = true distance (0-5) **PLUS** personality noise
- Player walks physically toward the ghost
- **Skill Required**: Player must observe the **pattern**, not just the level

#### EMF Pattern Recognition:
- **AGGRESSIVE**: Spikes early at distance (Banshee) OR spikes when moving fast close-up (Wraith)
  - **Skill**: Observe WHEN spike happens ‚Üí early = Banshee, close = Wraith
- **UNSTABLE**: Random jitter (Poltergeist) OR sine wave oscillation (Trickster)
  - **Skill**: Observe PATTERN ‚Üí random = Poltergeist, sine wave = Trickster
- **SHY**: Reads low until very close (Shade, Onyx)
  - **Skill**: Both similar, need other traits to distinguish
- **CALM**: Consistent, smooth readings (Phantom, Wraith)
  - **Skill**: Need other traits to distinguish

**Result**: EMF narrows to **2 ghosts** (never 1)

**Example**: Player sees "UNSTABLE pattern - random jitter"
- **Narrows to**: Poltergeist OR Trickster (2 ghosts)
- **Player notes**: "EMF: Unstable (random jitter)"

---

### 4. When EMF Peaks (4/5) ‚Üí Take a Photo
- Camera locks for **7 seconds** (film developing)
- This is the **intentional pacing mechanic**
- Player must wait, creating tension

---

### 5. While Camera is in Cooldown (7s Window) - **SKILL EXPRESSION**

Player rapidly rotates through tools to gather evidence:

#### 5a. EMF ‚Üí Double-Check Personality Pattern
- **Time**: 1-2 seconds
- **Action**: Observe pattern over time
- **Note**: "Random jitter" or "Sine wave" or "Spikes early"
- **Result**: Confirms EMF personality type

#### 5b. Spirit Box ‚Üí Tune Knobs, Get Words
- **Time**: 2-3 seconds (if quick tuning)
- **Action**: 
  - Adjust Knob A and Knob B
  - Get within tolerance ‚Üí Signal locks
  - Ghost whispers a word
- **Skill**: Fast, precise tuning
- **Note**: Word from ghost (e.g., "chaotic", "playful", "behind")
- **Result**: Word narrows to 2-3 ghosts (overlaps)

**Example**: Player tunes quickly, gets word "chaotic"
- **Narrows to**: Poltergeist OR Trickster (2 ghosts)
- **Player notes**: "Spirit Box: 'chaotic'"

#### 5c. Thermal ‚Üí Check Temperature Pattern
- **Time**: 1 second
- **Action**: Quick scan for cold spots
- **Skill**: Observe BEHAVIOR, not just reading
  - Stable cold spot?
  - Moving cold spot?
  - Appearing/disappearing?
- **Note**: "Cold Spot (stable)" or "Deep Cold (ground source)"
- **Result**: Thermal narrows to 2-3 ghosts

**Example**: Player sees "Cold Spot (appears/disappears randomly)"
- **Narrows to**: Poltergeist (random cold spots)
- **Player notes**: "Thermal: Cold Spot (unstable)"

#### 5d. Radar ‚Üí Verify Direction (Optional)
- **Time**: 0.5 seconds
- **Action**: Quick check if ghost moved
- **Result**: No deduction, just navigation

**Total Time**: ~5-7 seconds ‚úÖ **Perfect fit for camera cooldown!**

---

### 6. After Film Develops ‚Üí Check Photo
- Player checks the photo manifestation:
  - **FAINT_SILHOUETTE** ‚Üí Wraith OR Shade
  - **MOTION_BLUR** ‚Üí Poltergeist OR Trickster
  - **SCREAMING_FACE** ‚Üí Banshee OR Poltergeist
  - **INVISIBLE** ‚Üí Phantom OR Shade
  - **SHADOW_SILHOUETTE** ‚Üí Onyx OR Shade
  - **GLITCH_STREAKS** ‚Üí Trickster OR Poltergeist
  - **DEEP_SHADOW** ‚Üí Banshee OR Onyx
  - **HALF_FORMED_BODY** ‚Üí Shade OR Onyx

**Skill Required**: 
- Each ghost has 2 possible manifestations with different probabilities
- Player might need **multiple photos** to see both types
- Example: Banshee shows SCREAMING_FACE (85%) or DEEP_SHADOW (15%)
  - If player only sees DEEP_SHADOW, might think it's Onyx
  - Need multiple photos to be sure

**Example**: Player sees "MOTION_BLUR"
- **Narrows to**: Poltergeist OR Trickster (2 ghosts)
- **Player notes**: "Camera: Motion Blur"

---

### 7. Gather Enough Combined Hints ‚Üí Open Evidence Tab

Player opens the **Evidence Tab** (Phasmophobia-style checklist):

#### Evidence Tab Features:
- **EMF Personality Dropdown**: Select specific personality (Calm, Unstable, Shy, Aggressive)
- **Spirit Box Words Checkbox**: Present / Ruled Out / Unknown
- **Thermal Reading Dropdown**: Select specific reading (Normal, Cold Spot, Deep Cold)
- **Camera Manifestation Dropdown**: Select specific type (9 options)

#### Player Fills Out Evidence:
1. **EMF Personality**: Select "Unstable"
2. **Whispers**: Check "Present"
3. **Thermal Reading**: Select "Cold Spot"
4. **Camera Manifestation**: Select "Motion Blur"

#### Possible Ghosts Section Updates:
- Shows ghosts that match current evidence
- **Narrows down in real-time** as player selects evidence

### 8. Deduction Process - **SKILL-BASED PROBLEM SOLVING**

#### Example Case: Identifying Poltergeist

**Step 3**: Player takes photo

**Step 6**: **REALIZATION** - Check Whispers Status
- Player tries Spirit Box again: Can't get signal lock easily
- **Observation**: "Spirit Box is hard to tune, minimal communication"

## üß© Key Skill Requirements

### 1. **EMF Pattern Recognition**
- **Random Jitter** (Poltergeist) vs **Sine Wave** (Trickster)
- **Spikes Early** (Banshee) vs **Spikes When Close** (Wraith)
- **Skill**: Observe pattern over time, not just current reading

### 2. **Camera Manifestation Probability**
- Each ghost has 2 possible manifestations
- Player might need **multiple photos** to see both types
- **Skill**: Don't jump to conclusions from one photo

### 3. **Thermal Behavior Observation**
- **Stable** vs **Moving** vs **Appearing/Disappearing**
- **Ground Source** vs **Air Source**
- **Skill**: Observe behavior, not just reading type

### 4. **Spirit Box Word Combinations**
- Single words overlap (e.g., "behind" = Trickster OR Banshee)
- **Word combinations** are more unique
- **Skill**: Remember word pairs, not single words

### 5. **Evidence Tab Logic**
- Must combine 2-3 traits to identify
- **Skill**: Logical deduction, not just collecting evidence

### 6. **Trickster Contradiction Recognition** (Advanced)
- Trickster shows random, contradictory traits
- Evidence doesn't match any ghost definition
- **Skill**: Recognize "0 matches" = Trickster
- **Skill**: Know all ghost definitions to spot contradictions

## üéØ Minimum Evidence Required

**Every ghost requires 2-3 traits minimum to identify uniquely:**

- **Banshee**: AGGRESSIVE EMF + PRESENT Whispers (2 traits)
- **Phantom**: DEEP_COLD Thermal + CALM EMF (2 traits) OR DEEP_COLD + INVISIBLE (2 traits)
- **Trickster**: **0 matches = Trickster!** - **requires recognizing contradiction**
- **Wraith**: AGGRESSIVE EMF + RULED_OUT Whispers (2 traits)
- **Poltergeist**: UNSTABLE EMF (jitter) + RULED_OUT Whispers (2 traits) - **requires pattern recognition skill**
- **Shade**: SHY EMF + NORMAL Thermal (2 traits)
- **Onyx**: SHY EMF + DEEP_COLD Thermal (2 traits)

**No single trait identifies any ghost!**
