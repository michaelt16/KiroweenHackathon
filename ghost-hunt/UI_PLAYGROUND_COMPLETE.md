## üé® Visual Style Descriptions

### A: Clean Digital Classified OS
- Dark navy/black backgrounds (#0a0f14, #0f1a1f)
- Neon teal accents (#2dd4bf)
- Digital HUD elements
- Glowing effects and shadows
- System-ui fonts
- Professional tech aesthetic

### B: Analog Journal
- Brown/cream paper textures (#f5f1e8, #8B4513)
- Handwritten elements (Courier New)
- Sticky notes, tape, Polaroids
- Coffee stains and imperfections
- Physical artifacts aesthetic
- Warm, tactile feel

### C: Hybrid Tech + Analog
- Digital frames with paper content
- Neon borders (#2dd4bf) around analog elements
- Mix of system-ui and Courier New fonts
- Best of both worlds approach
- Balanced modern/vintage

### D: Paranormal Detective Bureau
- Sepia/brown tones (#2a2520, #d4c5a0)
- Case file aesthetic
- Classified stamps and labels
- Arial/sans-serif fonts
- 1950s government bureau feel
- Professional investigation vibe

### E: Explorer's Field Diary
- Aged paper (#f5ead8)
- Hand-drawn elements
- Compass roses and sketches
- Georgia serif fonts
- Victorian explorer aesthetic
- Leather-bound journal feel

### F: Modern Agent with Analog Notes
- Digital base (#0a0f14)
- Sticky notes and Polaroids as overlays
- Clean digital UI with analog accents
- Mix of modern and vintage
- Practical field agent aesthetic

### G: Premium Horror Notebook
- Clean white/cream (#faf8f5, #fffef9)
- Minimal elegant design
- Subtle shadows and borders
- Georgia serif fonts
- High-end notebook aesthetic
- Sophisticated and restrained

## üéØ Decision Framework

When choosing a style, consider:

- **Atmosphere**: Does it feel paranormal/spooky?
- **Usability**: Can you read it while walking?
- **Mobile-first**: Does it work on small screens?
- **Performance**: Can it be implemented efficiently?
- **Uniqueness**: Does it stand out from other apps?
- **Scalability**: Can it grow with new features?
